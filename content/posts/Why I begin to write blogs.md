So, why did I start writing a blog?

Because I want to write something to record what I think and what I want to do. 

I had this idea when I was a kid, even though I don't know what is "Blog" at that time. At that time, I had already realized that I would forget everything I thought if there are no media to record my ideas. The fact is I was right. 

Due to my lazy childhood, I lost memories of all of my creative and critical ideas about all aspects of my life:  my family, my friendships, my hobbies and so on. Although I couldn't recall any specific ideas, I firmly believe that they influenced my life. 

I should record them since they are parts of my life. I expected that one day, when I am older, like ten years or decades later, when I look back, I can read what I think when I was young. All of the ideas and thoughts are valuable, no matter they are silly, foolish or provoking. 

Inspired by my friends and many strangers, I decided to start my own blog. It is simple and crude now, but I will continue improving it for the rest of my life.

I will also record my class notes on Notes page.
